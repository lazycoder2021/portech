How We Work

Crafting websites is a four-step process: i) research ii) prototype iii) develop and iv) support. And since we also deal with website content and SEO, supporting your website becomes an absolute necessity.

Research

We first analyze your brand objective and then position it accordingly to your website visitors. Competitor analysis is also carried out.

Prototype

Based on our research, we later create a prototype that would suit your business requirements and hand it over to you for your comments.

Develop

Once you sign off the prototype, we develop your website by applying colors that reflect your brand's goals. And hand it over to you.

Support

Finally we offer continous support to your now live website by tweaking its content and SEO tactics until your website starts ranking well.

Our Services

Though ours is a 3-in-1 package service, it can still be split into 3 categories: i) web design / development ii) copywriting / content writing and iii) implementing SEO best practices.

Website Development

Designing website prototypes and developing fully functional websites from those designs is the first step in website development process.

Copy & Content Writing

Next step is writing appealing content that sits within these websites. Content that can be split into 2 categories: i) copywriting and ii) content writing. We deal with both.

SEO Best Practices

Final process is to tailor the developed website and make it appealing to search engines so that it gets noticed online. We do this by implementing SEO.
